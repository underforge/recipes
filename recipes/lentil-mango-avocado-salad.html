Lentil, Mango & Avocado Salad

Prep: 15 min Serves: 2 Vegan · GF

Ingredients

  • Salad
  • 1 canlentils, drained and rinsed
  • 2 mediumtomatoes, diced
  • 2avocados, diced
  • 1mango, diced
  • 3 inchescucumber, diced
  • 1 smallshallot, thinly sliced (or 3 Tbsp onion, finely minced)
  • 1 bunchcilantro, roughly chopped
  • salt, to taste
  • Tahini Dressing
  • 4 Tbsptahini
  • 4 Tbspolive oil
  • 5 Tbspfresh lemon juice
  • 2 Tbspmaple syrup
  • ½ clovegarlic, minced
  • salt, to taste

Instructions

  1. Drain and rinse the lentils. Pat dry if they seem wet.
  2. Dice the mango, avocado, tomato, and cucumber into similar-sized chunks. Thinly slice the shallot (or finely mince the onion). Roughly chop the basil.
  3. Whisk together all dressing ingredients until smooth. Taste and adjust salt or lemon as needed — the dressing amounts are a starting point, adjust to your preference.
  4. Combine all salad ingredients in a large bowl. Pour the dressing over and toss gently to coat.
  5. Serve immediately — the avocado will start to brown if it sits too long.
