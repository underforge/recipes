Montreal Bagels

Prep: 90 min Cook: 30 min Serves: 16 bagels Vegan option · DF
Original recipe: CBC / The Great Canadian Baking Show ↗

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • 350gwarm water
  • 60gsugar
  • 1 Tbspbrown sugar (optional)
  • 30goil
  • 10gactive dry yeast
  • 1egg
  • 20ghoney or syrup (optional)
  • 8gsalt
  • up to 700gflour (start with ~550g)
  • Honey Water
  • 350ghoney or syrup (optional)
  • Topping
  • generouslysesame seeds
  • generouslypoppy seeds

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 450°F (230°C) with a parchment-lined baking sheet inside — it needs to be hot when the bagels go in. Fill a large pot with water and bring to a boil over medium-high heat.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the warm water, sugar, oil, yeast, egg, and honey. Whisk in the salt. Gradually stir in flour (start with ~550g), one cup at a time, until a soft dough forms.
  3. Turn dough onto a surface and knead until it comes together, adding flour as needed to prevent sticking. Knead for 10–12 minutes until the dough is soft and springs back when you press it.
  4. Place dough in a lightly oiled bowl, turning to coat. Cover with a towel and rest for 30 minutes.
  5. Cut dough into 16 pieces (about 75g each). Keep pieces covered with a towel while you work.
  6. Roll each piece into a 10–12 inch rope — avoid adding flour unless necessary to prevent sticking. Wrap the rope around your four fingers, overlapping the ends on your palm to form a circle. Seal by rolling your hand back and forth with gentle pressure. Place on a floured surface and cover. Repeat with remaining pieces.
  7. Let shaped bagels rest for 30 minutes.
  8. Add the 350g honey or syrup (if using) to the pot of boiling water and bring back to a full boil. Gently lower bagels in batches and boil for 1½–2 minutes per side. Remove with a slotted spoon, place on a rack, and sprinkle heavily with sesame and poppy seeds while still wet.
  9. Place seeded bagels on the preheated pan in the oven. Bake for 15–20 minutes until deep golden brown.
