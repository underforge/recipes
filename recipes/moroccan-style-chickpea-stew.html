Moroccan-Style Chickpea Stew

Prep: 15 min Cook: 50 min Serves: 4 Vegan · GF option
Original recipe: Best of Vegan ↗

My Changes

Aaron's Modifications

  • Omitted Ras El Hanout — skipped entirely rather than substituting
  • Omitted Harissa — left out the heat

Ingredients

  • Base
  • 2 Tbspolive oil
  • 1 mediumonion, diced
  • 4 clovesgarlic, minced
  • 30 ozchickpeas, drained
  • 2 cups (330g)sweet potatoes, cubed
  • Spices
  • 1 tspsweet paprika
  • salt and pepper, to taste
  • Sauce
  • 2 Tbsptomato paste
  • 28 ozcrushed tomatoes
  • 1 cupvegetable broth
  • Finish
  • 5 ozbaby spinach, roughly chopped
  • 2 Tbspfresh parsley, chopped (garnish)
  • Serve With
  • couscous

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Sauté onions for 3–5 minutes until translucent. Add chickpeas and sweet potatoes; cook another 5–8 minutes.
  2. Add paprika, tomato paste, and garlic. Mix and cook 2–3 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes and broth. Lower heat to medium-low and simmer covered for 35–40 minutes until sweet potatoes are soft, stirring occasionally.
  4. Add spinach and cook until wilted. Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper.
  5. Serve warm over couscous and garnish with parsley.
