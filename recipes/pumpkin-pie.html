Pumpkin Pie

Prep: 15 min Cook: 50 min Serves: 8 Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • Crust
  • 1pie crust, store-bought or homemade
  • Filling
  • 1 canpumpkin purée
  • 2 largeeggs (or ¼ cup cornstarch for vegan)
  • ½ cupmilk
  • ½ cupmaple syrup
  • ¼ cupsugar
  • 1 tspground cinnamon
  • 1 tspground ginger
  • ¼ tspground cloves or allspice
  • ½ tspsalt
  • 1 tspvanilla extract
  • Streusel Topping
  • ¼ cupall-purpose flour
  • ¼ cupsugar
  • ¼ cupchopped nuts
  • 2 Tbspbutter, cold

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Press pie crust into a 9-inch pie dish and crimp the edges.
  2. Whisk together all filling ingredients until smooth. Pour into the unbaked crust.
  3. Make the streusel: combine flour, sugar, and chopped nuts, then cut in the cold butter with a fork or your fingers until it resembles coarse crumbles.
  4. Sprinkle the streusel evenly over the filling.
  5. Bake for 45–55 minutes until the filling is set — it should be mostly firm with just a slight jiggle in the centre. If the crust edges are browning too fast, cover them with foil.
  6. Cool completely on a rack before slicing. Refrigerate leftovers.
